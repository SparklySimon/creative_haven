ABOUT THE BUSINESS

Creative Haven is a welcoming arts and crafts shop designed for creators of all skill levels. We offer starter kits, guided instruction, and a peaceful, machine-free environment where you can explore your artistic side. With a wide range of color schemes and themes—from cute and festive to dark and gothic—our shop provides endless inspiration for every crafter.

Our Backstory

During our spare time, we learned origami with our parents friends. It was pretty boring at first; it basically involves folding a blank sheet of paper for about an entire hour. When time passes, it started to grow on us. As a result, we decided to sell our work for about a dollar for the whole world to appreciate. We also once invested in popsicle stick projects for the sake of either asthetics or utility. While it may seem more challenging, we also try painting in order to express ourselves. It was a fun thing to do for a hobby, and you might give it a chance someday.

Our Promise

At Creative Haven, we promise to provide a fun, stress-free, and inclusive space where anyone can learn, create, and express themselves through arts and crafts. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced artist, we are dedicated to offering the best materials, guidance, and support to help you bring your ideas to life.
